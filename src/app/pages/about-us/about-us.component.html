Our Journey

Technico was born out of our participation in the Careed Shifting Program by the OTE Group of Companies. Throughout this beautiful journey of learning and growth, we embarked on a capstone project that allowed us to piece together our skills and passion, creating something uniquely ours.

This project represents not just our technical abilities but also our commitment to innovation and excellence. We are excited to present Technico to our stakeholders, showcasing our dedication to transforming property management through modern solutions.
